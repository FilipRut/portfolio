problem:
  heading: "The Challenge"
  body: "Existing self-checkout kiosks had a 34% error rate requiring staff intervention. Elderly users avoided them entirely. The interface was designed for mouse-and-keyboard, not touch — tiny targets, nested menus, confusing produce lookup."

solution:
  heading: "The Solution"
  body: "Redesigned for touch-first with minimum 48px targets. Replaced produce lookup with visual grid (photos, not text lists). Added weight-based smart suggestions — place an apple on the scale, see apple varieties ranked by likelihood. One-tap age verification for alcohol via staff NFC badge."

results:
  heading: "Impact"
  body: "Pilot across 120 stores in Poland."
