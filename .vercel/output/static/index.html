About me

I'm Filip — a Lead AI UX/UI Designer and Webflow developer based in Warsaw. I help brands cut through corporate chaos with sharp design systems, rapid prototyping, and AI-augmented workflows that actually ship.

Over the past years I've led design for Orange, HP, Lidl, Enea and others — building everything from enterprise dashboards to consumer apps and self-checkout kiosks.

How I design with AI

Not replacing creativity.
Redirecting it.

01

Listen before prompting

Every project starts with the same question — what problem are we actually solving? AI doesn't change that. It just makes the cost of being wrong lower and the speed of finding right higher.

02

Think in systems, not screens

I map the full picture first — user flows, data architecture, edge cases. AI accelerates exploration of variants I wouldn't have time to sketch by hand. More paths tested, stronger decisions.

03

Prototype at the speed of thought

From concept to interactive prototype in hours, not weeks. AI handles the repetitive scaffolding so I stay focused on the craft — the micro-interactions, the typography, the feeling.

04

Validate with real signals

Beautiful doesn't mean useful. I test early, test often, and let data guide iteration. AI helps synthesize feedback faster — but the judgment call is always human.

05

Ship and keep shaping

Design doesn't end at handoff. I stay close to code, close to users, close to the product. The best work evolves after launch — when reality meets intention.

AI is my multiplier, not my replacement. It lets me spend less time on production and more time on the decisions that actually matter.
