Selgros B2B Platform

From paper catalogs to digital wholesale ordering

End-to-end B2B e-commerce platform for Selgros wholesale. Bulk ordering, credit management, and delivery scheduling for HoReCa businesses.

B2BE-commerceEnterprise
ClientSelgros
CompanyDeloitte Digital
RoleLead UX Designer
Team3 designers, 6 engineers, 2 PMs
Timeline12 months, 2022–2023
PlatformWeb (desktop-first, responsive)
ToolsFigma, Miro, Jira, Amplitude
DeliverablesPlatform UX, Design system, B2B component library

The Challenge

Selgros B2B clients (restaurants, hotels, caterers) were ordering via phone, fax, and paper catalogs. Digital adoption was under 8%. The existing web portal was a consumer e-commerce template forced onto B2B workflows — no bulk ordering, no credit terms, no recurring orders.

The Solution

Built purpose-designed B2B flows: quick-order pad (type SKU + quantity), saved order templates for weekly restocking, split delivery scheduling, and integrated credit line management. Every flow optimized for the 'I know what I need' user, not the browsing consumer.

Impact

Measured 6 months post-launch.

42% Digital order adoption (from 8%)
+€3.2M Monthly digital GMV
-55% Order processing time
89 NPS score
